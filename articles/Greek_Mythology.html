Introduction

Greek mythology consists of stories and legends about gods, heroes, and mythical creatures that have influenced Western literature and culture for centuries.

Major Gods and Goddesses

  • Zeus: King of the gods and ruler of Mount Olympus.
  • Hera: Queen of the gods and goddess of marriage.
  • Poseidon: God of the sea and earthquakes.
  • Athena: Goddess of wisdom and warfare.
  • Apollo: God of the sun, music, and prophecy.

Conclusion

Greek mythology remains a cornerstone of Western storytelling, shaping literature, movies, and philosophy.
